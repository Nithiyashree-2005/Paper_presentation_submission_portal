About IIT Madras

The Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras or IIT-M) is a public technical university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It is one of the eight public Institutes of Eminence of India and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance.

Founded in 1959 with assistance from the West German government, IITM was the third Indian Institute of Technology established by the Government of India. It consistently ranks as the best engineering institute in India by the NIRF since 2016.

The institute started with 120 students and has grown into a globally recognized center of excellence. IIT Madras maintains international collaborations and continues to be a leading contributor to education and research in engineering and technology.

It celebrated its Golden Jubilee in 2009 and Diamond Jubilee in 2019, marking decades of innovation, academic excellence, and societal contribution.
